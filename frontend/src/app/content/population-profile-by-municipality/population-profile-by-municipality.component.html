TECHNICAL NOTES


Population Density- refers to the number of people per unit of land area at a specific time period


Population Growth Rate (PGR)- the rate at which the population is increasing (or decreasing) due to natural increase and net migration, expressed as a percentage of the base population


Sex Ratio-the ratio of males to females in a given population expressed as the number of males per 100 females
